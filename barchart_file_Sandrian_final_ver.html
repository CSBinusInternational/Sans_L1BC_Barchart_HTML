The pi-chart represents the amount of people living in cabins A, B, C, D, E, and T [reading clockwise from dark red to light blue] in the group of people.

There were 4 in cabin A. There were 16 in cabin B. There were 15 in cabin C. There were 6 in cabin D. There were 4 in cabin E. There was 1 in cabin T.

Cabin T is the one outlier in the group of 60 people. Some data was incomplete therefore this does not total to 60 people.

Many of the passengers had siblings/spouses aboard the Titanic, this pi-chart showcases the amount of sibsp that the 60 people had.

Reading clockwise from dark red to pink are number of siblings/spouse of 0, 1, and 2. 43 people had 0 sibsp, 15 had 1 sibsp, and only 2 had 2 sibps.

In addition, the passengers also had parents/children aboard with them. This pi-chart shows the amount of parch that each of the 60 people had.

Reading clockwise from dark red to pink are number of parents/children of 0, 1, and 2. 45 people had 0 parch's, 7 had 1 parch's, and 8 had 2 parch's.
